How it works

How Dealality works

Dealality moves hotel opportunities through a more structured, confidential process—from intake and matching to comparison, shortlist, and secure engagement.

  1. 1
    Deal intake

    Owners submit the opportunity through a guided intake with deal scope, priorities, and filters.

  2. 2
    Brand and operator matching

    The platform aligns opportunities to relevant brand and operator profiles based on fit.

  3. 3
    Owner-led outreach

    Owners select which parties to engage, and the platform notifies them to respond.

  4. 4
    Pre-LOI comparison

    Commercial terms are submitted in a structured format so owners can compare and shortlist more effectively.

  5. 5
    Deal room launch

    Finalists move into a secure deal room where materials are shared and confidentiality agreements are executed.

  6. 6
    Final negotiation and close

    Once aligned, parties move offline for agreement drafting, legal review, and final negotiation.
