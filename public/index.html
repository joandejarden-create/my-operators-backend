How The Platform Works

Dealality reflects how hotel transactions get done—privately and confidentially. Unlike public listing sites, owners retain total control over whom, how, and when they approach the market. From submission to signing, seamlessly.

  1. 1Deal intake: Owner submits project via guided intake; platform captures scope, filters, and goals.
  2. 2Brand/operator matching: Our engine scores and aligns opportunities with brand/operator profiles.
  3. 3Owner offer request: Owner selects which brands/operators to engage; platform notifies them to respond.
  4. 4Pre-LOI term comparison: Brands submit commercial terms in a structured format; owner compares and shortlists finalists.
  5. 5Deal room launch: Platform opens secure virtual room for finalists; NDAs executed, confidential materials shared.
  6. 6Platform exit: Brand and owner move offline for MA/FA drafting and legal steps—readiness confirmed.

Tools That Drive Smarter, Faster Deal Wins

Purpose-built for hospitality. Every feature designed to remove friction and accelerate decisions.

Smart Matching

Match deals to your brand profile based on size, segment, and market. Standardized intake and scoring help you focus on viable opportunities—ordered by relevance.

Virtual Deal Rooms + NDA

Secure space for confidential materials (brochures, PIP, pro formas) only accessible after NDAs are signed digitally through the platform. All sending is direct—no public listing or deal-searching capabilities.

AI Deal Summaries

Auto-generates clean, executive-ready one-page deal teasers based on owner submissions. Save hours of manual data compilation and get to the right conversation faster.

Brand Comparison Tool

Side-by-side comparison of key brand terms: initial fees, royalty percentages, PIP requirements, territory protections. Owners compare offers; brands see where they stand.

Franchise Fee Estimators

Estimates total franchise costs based on brand parameters and owner-provided revenue assumptions. Reduces speculative feasibility work and gives owners clarity before they engage.

Pipeline Reporting + Deal Heatmaps

Track all viewed, active, and declined deals; post private comments; assign internal leads—all in-platform. Deal activity heatmaps visualize flow by geography, brand fit, and status. Future: CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, custom systems).

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dealality. Still have questions? Contact us directly.

What is Dealality?

Dealality is the hospitality industry's first confidential, end-to-end deal platform that connects vetted hotel opportunities with qualified brands, operators, and investors. We provide smart matching, AI-generated deal summaries, virtual deal rooms with embedded NDA workflows, and dynamic pipeline tracking—all in a secure, purpose-built ecosystem.

How does the matching process work?

Our intelligent matching engine scores opportunities against brand profiles based on size, segment, market preferences, and strategic criteria. Owners submit their project once, and the platform automatically surfaces relevant brands and operators who meet their goals—no more repeating your story to dozens of unqualified prospects.

Is my deal information confidential?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is built into every layer of the platform. All connections require NDA execution before sensitive materials are shared. You control who sees your deal, when they see it, and what information to share at every stage. Optional "blind teaser" posting keeps your identity protected until you're ready to engage.

What are the fees for owners?

Dealality operates on a success-based model: $0 to post your project, and fees are only due when you sign an LOI with a selected brand or operator. Our per-key fee model ($125–$500+ depending on chain scale) scales with your project size. 90% of the fee is due at LOI signing, 10% at executed agreement. No upfront consulting fees like traditional advisors who charge $25K–$50K+ before results.

Can I integrate this with my existing CRM?

Yes! Dealality supports seamless integration with leading CRM systems including Salesforce, HubSpot, and custom platforms. Our API enables you to sync deal activity, pipeline tracking, and contact information directly with your existing workflows. Future enhancements include expanded webhook support and custom field mapping.

How long does it take to get matched with brands?

Once your project passes our verification process (typically 24-48 hours), the platform immediately begins matching you with relevant brands and operators. You can review matched profiles and select whom to engage within days—not the 6-12 weeks traditional consultants require. Our digital-first approach delivers shortlists faster while maintaining quality and confidentiality.
