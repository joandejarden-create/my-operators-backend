Let’s Capture Your Hotel Deal Opportunity

Instructions

Welcome to Dealality. This guided intake form will create a concise deal summary that our team and select brand/management partners use to evaluate your project and propose tailored terms. You can start with what you know today and refine details later.

Estimated time to complete: 15–20 minutes

What you’ll need

Before you start, it helps to have:

  • Basic property details (location, approximate room count, stage of development)
  • Your deal goals and preferences (brand/operator preferences, ownership or capital goals)
  • Any current encumbrances or obligations (existing management, franchise, or other agreements)

Tips for completing the form

  • Use plain language and your best estimates — exact numbers aren’t required for every question.
  • If something doesn’t apply or you’re unsure, select or write “N/A” or “Not sure”.
  • Upload any supporting documents you have (e.g. renderings, plans, financials) — you can add more later.
  • You can save as a draft and return from My Deals at any time before sharing with brands.

Final step

After completing the form:

  • Review your answers and click Submit to finalize this version of your deal summary.
  • Nothing is shared with brands until you choose to engage them through Dealality.
  • A member of our team will contact you within 1–3 business days to review your deal and recommended next steps.
