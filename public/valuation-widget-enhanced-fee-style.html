Methodology & Assumptions ▼

Revenue: ADR × Occupancy × 365 (annual rooms revenue).

NOI: Revenue × (1 − opex ratio). Opex by brand: Luxury 67–68%, Upscale 72–73%, Midscale 77–78%, Economy 78–80%.

Valuation: NOI ÷ cap rate. Cap rates: Luxury ~5.5%, Upscale ~6.75%, Midscale ~7–7.25%, Economy ~7.75–8.25%. Market context adjusts ±25 bps.

Yield on Cost: NOI ÷ (price per key + PIP). Includes conversion cost when entered.

IRR: Simplified multi-year projection using hold period, NOI growth assumption, and exit cap rate.

Sources: Industry benchmarks, public disclosures, Dealality data. Not a substitute for professional analysis.

Assumptions updated quarterly.
