DEAL CAPTURE™ MEMBERSHIP ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Dealality is a private-access platform designed to connect qualified hospitality players across North America, Latin America, the Caribbean, Middle East, and Europe. Membership is limited to those actively involved in hotel development, brand expansion, or operational growth. You must meet one or more of the following criteria:

CAPITAL-SIDE MEMBERS

(Hotel Owners, Developers, Investors, and Capital Platforms)

  • You own, operate, or control one or more hotel or resort assets generating at least $1M in annual revenue, or are actively developing a hotel or mixed-use project.
  • You are seeking a qualified brand or operator partner for a new build, conversion, or repositioning project within the next 24 months.
  • You represent a real estate investment group, family office, developer, or capital platform with a clear hospitality strategy and an active pipeline of projects.

Operator-Side Members

(Hotel Brands, Soft Brands, and Third-Party Management Companies)

  • You are a hotel brand, soft brand, or independent operator actively expanding in the Americas, Middle East or Europe, with a focus on franchise or management agreements.
  • You have executed at least 3 qualified hotel agreements in the last 3 years, or can demonstrate current growth momentum and delivery capability.
  • You offer a clear value proposition and support model for hotel owners and can engage in structured deal conversations.

Advisory & Support Members

(Consultants, Legal Advisors, Technical Experts)

You are a consultant or advisor with a proven track record supporting hotel projects, and have been directly engaged in helping owners or operators identify and structure brand or operator partnerships.
